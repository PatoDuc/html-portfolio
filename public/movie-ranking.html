The Best movies According to Patricio

My top 3 movies of all time.

Rango

Everthing of it is esentially perfect, from the vissuals to the soundtrack. The message and the character development. It has a Clint Eastwood cameo and Johnny Depp stars in it.

Inception

It has one of the best endings of all time, followed by a wonderful score and an stunning cast with Di Caprio, Page, Hardy, and Cillian Murphy.

Goodfellas

Martin Scorcese's masterpiece. A wonderful story about the New York mob with De Niro & Pesci. A masterclass at storytelling and a truly funny letter of love to cinema.
